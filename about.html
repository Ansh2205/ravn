A little bit about us 📖

How a group chat turned into a second home.

We started RAVN because we were honestly just tired of the typical college club scene. Too much stress, too much politics, and not enough actual fun. We wanted a space where we could just grab some pizzas, watch terrible movies, and plan random weekend trips without overthinking it.

The house rules 📜

What makes our community tick.

🤝

Zero "Networking"

Leave the resumes at the door. We aren't here to swap business cards or talk about GPAs. We're here to talk about our favorite TV shows, debate whether pineapple belongs on pizza, and just make actual friends.

🧳

Saying 'Yes' to Plans

Someone dropped a link to a weird indie movie in the group chat? We're going. Found a cheap cabin two hours away? We're packing the cars. Life's better when you're spontaneous.

🛋️

Creating a Safe Haven

College and work can get incredibly overwhelming. RAVN is designed to be the comfortable couch you crash on at the end of a long week. It's a drama-free, stress-free zone where everyone looks out for each other.
