House Rules & Privacy

Effective: June 17, 2026 • v2.1.0

Welcome to the RAVN Clubhouse. To ensure a safe, highly professional, and deeply enjoyable environment for all our student members, innovators, and public guests, we require everyone to adhere to the following operational guidelines. By accessing our platform, utilizing our network, or attending our events, you formally agree to these terms.

Part 1: The House Rules

Community Guidelines & Conduct

1. Code of Conduct & Ethics

The RAVN ecosystem thrives on mutual respect. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for behaviors that disrupt the community.

  • Respect & Inclusivity: RAVN is an inclusive student community. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or abusive behavior towards any member, guest, or administrative staff will result in an immediate, unappealable ban from the network.
  • Professionalism: Members are expected to maintain professional conduct during all official events, workshops, and meetups. You represent the club.
  • Substance Policy: Unless explicitly stated for specific vetted venues, the consumption or distribution of illegal substances at RAVN events is strictly prohibited and will result in involvement of local authorities.

2. Event Registration & Digital Passes

Our infrastructure relies on accurate headcounts to deliver premium experiences.

  • Mandatory RSVP: All attendees must RSVP through the official RAVN platform. Walk-ins without prior digital registration may be denied entry due to venue capacity limits and security protocols.
  • Digital Pass Security: Your auto-generated Digital Pass (QR Code) is strictly non-transferable. Our door scanners ping live databases; screenshots shared with non-members will be flagged. You must present the dynamic pass along with a valid College/Student ID at the venue gates.
  • The Cancellation Courtesy: If you RSVP for an event but can no longer attend, you must cancel via the member portal or notify the administration at least 24 hours in advance. Repeated "no-shows" will temporarily restrict your account from RSVPing to future high-demand events.

3. Payments & Financial Integrity

We process all finances transparently through industry-standard gateways.

  • Gateway Security: All payments for memberships, subscriptions, and premium events are processed securely via Razorpay. RAVN does not store any card details, UPI PINs, or raw bank data on our servers.
  • Refund Policy: Due to the upfront costs of venue bookings and logistics, refunds are generally not provided for event tickets or memberships unless an event is officially canceled or heavily rescheduled by RAVN Administration.
  • Financial Fraud: Fraudulent chargebacks, forged UPI transaction IDs, or attempting to bypass the payment verification system will result in permanent expulsion from the club and reporting to relevant financial institutions.

Part 2: Privacy Policy

Data Collection & Security

1. Information We Collect

When you register for a RAVN membership or RSVP for an event, we collect minimal necessary operational data:

  • Identity Metrics: Full Name, Age, Gender, and Educational Institution.
  • Contact Vectors: Email address and Phone number (for vital event communications and account recovery).
  • Transaction Data: Cryptographic payment references (e.g., Razorpay Order IDs and Signatures).
  • System Logs: Basic device data and IP logs strictly for security monitoring and abuse prevention.

2. How We Utilize Your Data

Your data is strictly localized for club operations and improving your member experience:

  • Generating and cryptographically signing your unique Digital Member Pass.
  • Sending automated event updates, RSVP confirmations, and password reset protocols via our secure EmailJS provider.
  • Verifying your identity dynamically at venue checkpoints via the RAVN Scanner.

3. Security Infrastructure & Sharing

We treat your digital footprint with the highest level of respect.

  • Zero Third-Party Selling: RAVN will never sell, rent, trade, or expose your personal information to third-party marketers, advertisers, or data brokers.
  • Cryptographic Storage: Member passwords are cryptographically hashed using industry-standard `bcrypt` algorithms before being stored in our MongoDB Atlas clusters. The core administration team cannot see your password.
  • Trusted Service Providers: We share minimal, compartmentalized data with trusted service providers (such as EmailJS for notifications and Razorpay for payment processing) strictly for functional execution.

4. Your Digital Rights & Contact

As a member of the network, you have absolute control over your digital footprint.

  • You maintain the right to request a complete export of your data profile.
  • You maintain the right to request full account deletion ("The Right to be Forgotten"), which will permanently wipe your identity from our active operational databases.
